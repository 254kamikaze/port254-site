Getting Started with port254

From zero to protected in 15 minutes

Browse the Detection Catalog

Start by finding threats relevant to your environment. We have detections for S7comm (Siemens PLCs), Modbus (SCADA), DNP3 (electrical substations), and financial fraud patterns.

ðŸ’¡ Pro Tip: Start with CRITICAL severity detections for your protocol. For example, if you use Siemens PLCs, search for "S7" and look for red badges.

Review Detection Details

Click any detection card to see the full details. You'll see exactly what it detects, why it matters, and how it maps to compliance frameworks.

Test in Lab Environment

Before deploying to production, test the detection safely. Our lab sends synthetic test events to your Elasticsearch cluster to prove the rule works.

Export Detection Rule

Once you've tested and verified the detection works, export it in your SIEM's native format. One-click export means you don't have to manually translate the logic.

Deploy to Production SIEM

Take the exported rule and import it into your production SIEM. The exact steps vary by tool, but the concept is the same: create a new detection rule and paste the query.

Monitor & Respond

Now your detection is live. When it fires, you'll get real-time alerts about potential threats to your industrial systems.
