About Port254

Specialist OT cybersecurity advisory helping critical infrastructure operators secure industrial systems against real-world threats.

What We Do

Port254 provides OT cybersecurity advisory services to operators of critical infrastructure across energy, water, transport, and manufacturing sectors. We help organisations understand their OT risk posture, meet regulatory obligations, and build lasting security capability.

Assess

Structured OT security assessments against IEC 62443, identifying gaps between current posture and target security levels across zones, conduits, and system components.

Advise

Translating complex technical risk into clear, actionable guidance for executives, boards, and operational teams. Risk-prioritised roadmaps that align security investment with business outcomes.

Build

Developing governance frameworks, policies, and incident response capabilities that meet regulatory requirements and stand up to scrutiny under the SOCI Act.

Our Approach

Every engagement is grounded in practical experience securing operational technology environments. We combine deep technical expertise with an understanding of how industrial operations actually work.

Practitioner-Led

Our advisory team holds IEC 62443, CISSP, OSCP, and OSEP certifications with hands-on experience across OT/ICS security assessments, offensive security, and governance frameworks.

Standards-Aligned

Assessments and recommendations are anchored to IEC 62443, mapped to regulatory obligations under the SOCI Act, and consistent with international best practice.

Threat-Informed

We operate live OT honeypot infrastructure monitoring five industrial protocols, generating real-world threat intelligence that directly informs our advisory work.

Sectors

We work with operators across critical infrastructure sectors where OT security directly impacts safety, service delivery, and national resilience.

Energy & Utilities

Power generation, transmission, distribution, and gas networks. SCADA, DCS, and energy management system security.

Water & Wastewater

Water treatment, distribution, and wastewater management systems. Process control and remote site security.

Transport & Logistics

Rail, port, and transport network control systems. Signalling, fleet management, and operational technology security.

Manufacturing

Industrial automation, production control, and supply chain systems. PLC, HMI, and industrial network security.
