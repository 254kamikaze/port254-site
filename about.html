Tony

OT Cybersecurity Advisor

I specialise in securing operational technology environments across critical infrastructure sectors including energy, water, transport, and manufacturing. My work sits at the intersection of industrial control systems, cybersecurity governance, and threat intelligence — translating complex technical risk into actionable outcomes for operators and executives.

With experience spanning OT/ICS security assessments, IEC 62443 implementation, and offensive security, I bring a practitioner's perspective to every engagement. I've worked across Australia and internationally, helping organisations align their OT security programs with regulatory frameworks including the SOCI Act and Kenya's Critical Information Infrastructure Regulations.

Port254 is my independent platform for advisory services, research, and threat intelligence. The honeypot infrastructure you'll find in the Lab section generates real-world data from industrial protocol honeypots — providing practical insight into how adversaries target OT environments.
